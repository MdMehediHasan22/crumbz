Crumbz About Us

Crumbz Cookies is a heartwarming concept born out of friendship, shared values, and a love for food. Brigitte Nielsen and Debbie Wingham, two individuals with strong ethical beliefs, public personas, and deep family values, formed a bond that centered around their mutual appreciation for good food and togetherness. Debbie, known for her grand cakes, would often create extravagant desserts. However, Brigitte, a keen baker, challenged her to create something more shareable and modest in size, embodying the idea that "sharing is caring." This led to the early stages of the Crumbz Cookie that combined the best of both worlds: a cookie that could be enjoyed warm and gooey or chilled and sliceable. Brigitte's love for baking, particularly her signature brownies, which have an element of cake and undertone of cookies, inspired Debbie to adapt one of her favorite cookie recipes, resulting in the cookie-cake hybrid. Dubbed the countess of confection by Food Network and Known for her grand cake confections it made sense that Debbie would bring some element of cake to the concept and after unveiling the Crumbz Cookies to Brigitte, the duo began experimenting with flavours and adding more layers and textures to the cookie dough recipe that Debbie had previously formulated, including nods to nostalgic flavours, using local seasonal fruits and produce and creating some on trend flavour profiles for the younger cookie eater. It was also important to the duo to introduce cookies that suited every palette and even the more health conscious amongst us, including dairy free, gluten free and natural sugars. This collaborative effort gave birth to Crumbz Cookies, a brand that emphasizes ethically sourced ingredients, and eco-conscious packaging. Crumbz Cookies is a testament to Brigitte and Debbie's humble beginnings and their commitment to creating a product that is accessible and enjoyable for everyone, staying true to their roots and love for family. The brand not only offers a delightful treat but also embodies the values of friendship, sharing, and sustainability.
