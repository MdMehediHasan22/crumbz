Crumbz Events

Crumbz Events specialises in seamless, bespoke children's party planning. From baby showers, birthdays and christenings to gender reveals. Our founders are globally recognised tastemakers, bringing their unique expertise to the table. Brigitte Nielsen, a Hollywood style icon and a working mother who has masterfully balanced her career in the public eye with planning countless family celebrations, she is big on family and as always gone to the next level to bring memorable moments to her loved ones, she as joined forces with Debbie Wingham, a dear friend and fellow working mother, celebrated in the media for her extraordinary art pieces and grand confections, dubbed the Countess of confection by Food Network and one of the top ten cake artists in the world. Together, they are revolutionising the event scene on the Costa del Sol. Whether you’re planning an intimate gathering with a more simplistic charm or a grand celebration with all the bells and whistles, we provide tailored packages to suit every budget starting from €450. Bringing wow factor to your child’s special day, ensuring an abundance of Instagram-worthy moments and cherished memories that will last a lifetime We create stunning table settings, for all age groups and themes with innovative goody bags, and unique event favours, alongside offering delicious, themed tea parties or individual lunch boxes made with themed sandwich shapes, savoury bites, and our sensational Crumbz Cookie collections, which are also customisable. All our ingridents are locally sourced and organic.
The full list of services include:
-Bespoke design and styling
-Beautifully styled luxury picnics and tea parties
-innovative props
-Balloon installations and garlands
-Invitations, stationery and personalised paperie
-Creative workshops to suit an array of age groups from cookie decorating, Painting and fashion design
-Party bags and favours
-Celebration cakes and our Crumbz Cookies
-Grazing tables & Luxury lunch boxes for the kids, and we even offer adult charcuterie displays for events where the adults are invited to stay
